Frequently Asked Questions

01 What markets does SmartStocks support?

SmartStocks currently supports all major US stock exchanges (NYSE, NASDAQ) and cryptocurrency markets. We're continuously adding support for international markets and additional asset classes.

02 How do I connect my brokerage account?

You can securely connect most major brokerage accounts via API or manually import your portfolio. We support TD Ameritrade, E*TRADE, Fidelity, and more with read-only access for security.

03 What security measures protect my data?

We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, two-factor authentication, and never store your brokerage login credentials. Your data remains secure with regular security audits and SOC 2 compliance.

04 Can I try SmartStocks before purchasing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose from our flexible subscription plans.
