Frequently Asked Questions

01 What types of businesses is SmartStocks designed for?

SmartStocks is perfect for retail stores, supermarkets, pharmacies, restaurants, and wholesalers. Our system adapts to manage any type of physical inventory, from clothing to perishable goods.

02 Does SmartStocks work offline?

Yes! SmartStocks has full offline functionality. All your sales and inventory changes are recorded locally and automatically sync when internet connection is restored.

03 Can multiple employees use the system?

Absolutely. SmartStocks supports multi-user access with role-based permissions. You can set different access levels for cashiers, managers, and administrators.

04 What hardware do I need to use SmartStocks?

SmartStocks runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux systems with minimum 4GB RAM. For retail use, we recommend adding a barcode scanner and receipt printer (both optional).

05 Is training included with purchase?

Yes, we provide comprehensive video tutorials and documentation. For businesses in Cameroon, we also offer in-person training sessions in major cities.
